Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects do you offer tutoring in?

We offer tutoring in all subjects for students in grades K-12, including math, science, English, history, and more. We also offer specialized tutoring for AP courses and standardized tests.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online tutoring options to provide flexibility for our students.

What is your teaching methodology?

Our teaching methodology is based on personalized instruction, individualized learning plans, and a supportive learning environment.

How do I enroll my child?

You can enroll your child by contacting us through our website or by calling our office. We will schedule a consultation to discuss your child's needs and create a customized learning plan.

What are your rates?

Our rates vary depending on the program and the number of hours per week. Please contact us for more information about our pricing.
